CANCER CURE

The Indian Cancer Society–Cancer Cure Fund provides financial support for cancer treatment to underprivileged patients. Since many cancers are curable if treated early, the fund has helped thousands nationwide return to healthy, productive lives. Notably, 45% of beneficiaries are between 16–45 years of age.

This first-of-its-kind initiative is run in partnership with HDFC Mutual Fund, which launched a debt fund in 2011 allowing investors to donate 50% or now 75% of their dividend income to ICS. Their continued support has enabled lifesaving treatment for thousands of patients, and ICS remains deeply grateful to all contributors.

Patient’s eligibility criteria:
  • Family income should not exceed Rs 4 lakh per year
  • Patient should be treated at ICS empanelled hospital only
  • Patients should be registered under general category
  • Projected survival rate 50% for adult patients and 60% for pediatric patients
  • Only for Indian citizens
Sanction criteria:
  • Maximum sanction Rs 5 lakhs and for Bone Marrow Transplant Rs 8 lakh
Treatment protocol covered:
  • Chemotherapy
  • Radiation
  • Surgery
  • Supportive care
  • Prosthesis
