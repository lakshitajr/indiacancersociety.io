Why Us?

We at the Indian Cancer Society are one of the oldest Cancer NGOs in India. Our work encompasses the entire Cancer spectrum from Cancer awareness, early detection, diagnosis and treatment funding, post-treatment support, and rehabilitation. Our main focus is the underprivileged section of the society both in urban and rural India.

Your one decision to donate to an NGO can save many lives. Thank you for choosing to donate to the Indian Cancer Society. Your contribution is valuable to us.

Who we are

The Indian Cancer Society (ICS), founded in 1951, is India‚Äôs first and oldest non-profit dedicated to comprehensive cancer care. We work across the entire cancer journey ‚Äî from awareness and early detection to treatment support, rehabilitation, and survivorship. Our goal is to make quality cancer care accessible to every individual, especially those from underserved and low-income communities, through reliable information, financial aid, and compassionate support.

Our Impact

ICS conducts large-scale awareness initiatives and screening camps that help detect cancer early, saving thousands of lives every year. We offer financial assistance for treatment, rehabilitation services, and emotional support to help patients rebuild their lives. Our cancer registries and data-led programs strengthen research, improve care outcomes, and guide national health policies. Through our continuous efforts, we reach communities across India and make cancer prevention and treatment more accessible.
