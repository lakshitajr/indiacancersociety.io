Painting a Future - Art Against Cancer

The Indian Cancer Society, founded in 1951 by Mr. Naval Tata and Dr. D. J. Jussawalla, has led India’s fight against cancer for 73 years. As we celebrate our 74th Founders Day, we proudly present “Painting a Future Art Against Cancer,” an exhibition of contemporary Indian art. Each curated piece carries a meaningful message and visual appeal. We invite art lovers to support this initiative. Your purchase not only brings home beautiful artwork but also helps underprivileged cancer patients and survivors. All proceeds will advance ICS’s mission across the cancer care continuum. For more details, please write to us at sonali.b@indiancancersociety.org or contact on +91-99673 64938
