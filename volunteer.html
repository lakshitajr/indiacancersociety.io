VOLUNTEER WITH US

Internship in Psycho Social Oncology at Indian Cancer Society, Mumbai

Internship Curriculum
  • Plan and maintain quality of sessions.
  • Coordinating the workshop/programs organized by ICS internship vertical.
  • Observing and coordinating monthly support group meetings.
  • Fun and supportive work environment.
  • An experiential understanding of social work.

Interns are expected to:

  • Give update once in a week.
  • Report has to be provided after completion of the internship.
  • Certificate will be provided.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Qualification: MSC (Clinical, Counseling or Health/Medical Psychology) final year student
  • Expertise in counseling
  • Experience in innovative research design
  • Fluent in Hindi/Marathi & English
  • Computer proficiency with MS Office suite
  • Duration: Minimum 1 Month
  • Time: Monday to Friday (10 am to 6 pm)
  • Number of vacancies: 2
Required documents:
  • Bonafide certificate/Recommendation letter from the Institute
  • Result of the last exam passed
  • Resume (latest version)

Please send your application to chitra@indiancancersociety.org

  • Statistical Data Analysis and Interpretation
  • Preparing drafts for research articles

  • Benefits: Exposure to research work and development of analytical skills
  • Certificate will be provided
  • Duration: Minimum 2 months
  • Time: Monday - Friday: 10 am to 6 pm (or mutually considered)
  • Location: Indian Cancer Society, 74 Jerbai Wadia Road, Bhowaeda, Parel, Mumbai 400012
